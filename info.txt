- Servers created with Seros may take up to 10 minutes to be able to access
- Servers created with Seros can only be opened through Seros for
  the first time on the device they were created on. After it has been connected to
  once, it will be available for connection on all your devices with Seros.
- Seros may not be able to connect to certain servers if they were created
  outside of Seros. It may be necessary for you to manually input the password
  a single time, and then Seros will be able to connect to it any time after.

- Servers created with Mercor Connect may take up to 10 minutes to be able to access
- Servers created with Mercor Connect can only be opened through Mercor Connect for
  the first time on the device they were created on. After it has been connected to
  once, it will be available for connection on all your devices with Mercor Connect.
- Mercor Connect may not be able to connect to certain servers if they were created
  outside of Mercor Connect. It may be necessary for you to manually input the password
  a single time, and then Mercor Connect will be able to connect to it any time after.
